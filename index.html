About Me

Hi, I'm Elon Musk: self-made billionaire, except for the emerald mine part. I've dedicated my life to saving humanity by selling it flamethrowers, launching cars into space for absolutely no reason, and buying the internet's favorite bird app just to make it slightly worse. I'm also a part-time rocket scientist, full-time meme lord, and occasional father to approximately 47 children, all of whom have names that sound like Wi-Fi passwords. I speak truth to power,which is easy when you are the power, and also when you own the platform where truth is spoken.

Now, about the Epstein thing: yes, my name appeared in those documents, but honestly, whose didn't? It was basically the LinkedIn of the early 2000s for wealthy men who "just wanted to network." Very exclusive club. Great catering, I'm told. I've since moved on to more reputable social circles, like world governments, shadowy think tanks, and the comment sections of my own posts where I argue with teenagers at 2 AM. Anyway, I'm laser-focused on the future: Mars, AI, brain chips, because the farther humanity looks forward, the less it looks back. And that, my friends, is just good business strategy. 🚀
